Vietnamese Egg Coffee

$5.00

Egg Coffee is a famous Vietnamese drink that originated in Hanoi in the 1940s. It is made by pouring strong Vietnamese coffee under a creamy foam of whipped egg yolk, sugar, and condensed milk. The drink has a rich, smooth texture similar to dessert custard, balancing the bold bitterness of coffee with a sweet and silky finish.
